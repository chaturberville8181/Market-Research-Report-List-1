<p><p>Aramid fibre reinforced plastics (AFRP) are composite materials that enhance the mechanical properties of plastics through the incorporation of aramid fibres. There are two primary types: reinforced thermosetting plastics (RTP) and reinforced thermoplastics (RTP). Reinforced thermosetting plastics are characterized by irreversible hardening upon curing, providing superior heat resistance and structural integrity, making them ideal for applications like automotive and aerospace. In contrast, reinforced thermoplastics can be remelted and reshaped, offering flexibility and ease of manufacturing for consumer goods and industrial applications.</p></p>
