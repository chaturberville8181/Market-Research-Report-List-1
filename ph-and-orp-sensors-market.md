<p><p>The pH and ORP sensors market consists of two primary types: insertion sensors and immersion sensors. Insertion pH and ORP sensors are designed for mounting directly into pipes or tanks, allowing for real-time monitoring within flowing systems. In contrast, immersion sensors are fully submerged in liquids, providing accurate readings in larger bodies of water or solutions. Both types are essential for various applications, including water treatment, industrial processes, and environmental monitoring, ensuring optimal chemical balances and system efficiency.</p></p>
