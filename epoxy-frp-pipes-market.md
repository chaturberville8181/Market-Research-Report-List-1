<p><p>The Epoxy Fiber Reinforced Polymer (FRP) pipes market primarily includes hot rolled steel pipes and cold drawn pipes. Hot rolled steel pipes are formed at high temperatures, offering greater flexibility and strength, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, cold drawn pipes undergo a process at room temperature, resulting in a finer finish and enhanced dimensional accuracy. Both types serve various industries, offering benefits such as corrosion resistance, lightweight construction, and improved durability for fluid transport.</p></p>
