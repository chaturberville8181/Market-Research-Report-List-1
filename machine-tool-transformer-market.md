<p><p>The Machine Tool Transformer market is primarily divided into two types: single-phase and three-phase transformers. Single-phase machine tool transformers are used in lighter applications, delivering power to smaller machines and tools, ideal for residential and light industrial settings. In contrast, three-phase machine tool transformers are suited for heavier industrial applications, providing greater power and efficiency to larger machinery. Both types are essential for ensuring stable voltage and optimizing performance in various machining processes.</p></p>
