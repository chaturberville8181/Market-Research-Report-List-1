<p><p>Air grippers are specialized devices used in automation and robotics to hold and manipulate objects. The market features three main types: </p><p>1. **Friction Grip** uses surface contact and force to grasp items, ideal for smooth or flat objects.</p><p>2. **Cradled Grip** securely supports items by conforming to their shape, suited for irregular or delicate parts. </p><p>3. **Encapsulated Grip** surrounds and envelops objects, providing extra security and protection, especially for fragile items. Each type serves distinct operational needs in various industries.</p></p>
