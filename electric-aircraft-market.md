<p><ul><li>Pure Electric</li><li>Hybrid Power</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric aircraft market is primarily categorized into two types: pure electric and hybrid power. Pure electric aircraft rely solely on electric engines, using batteries for propulsion, resulting in zero emissions during flight. They are ideal for short-range flights and urban air mobility. In contrast, hybrid power aircraft combine traditional combustion engines with electric propulsion, enhancing range and efficiency while reducing fuel consumption and emissions. This hybrid approach caters to longer distances, bridging the gap between conventional and fully electric aviation.</p></p>
