<p><ul><li>Wafer Tester</li><li>Packaged Device Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IC testers market comprises two primary segments: wafer testers and packaged device testers. Wafer testers evaluate semiconductor wafers during manufacturing to ensure functionality and performance before dicing. They are crucial for early defect detection and yield improvement. Packaged device testers, on the other hand, test semiconductor devices post-packaging to confirm that they operate correctly in their final form. This segment focuses on performance validation and robustness, ensuring devices meet specifications for various applications. Both types are vital for maintaining quality in the IC supply chain.</p></p>
