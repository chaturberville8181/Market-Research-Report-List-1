<p><p>The Helicobacter pylori testing kit market comprises two primary types: antibody tests and antigen tests. Antibody tests detect antibodies in the blood, which indicate a past or current infection with H. pylori. In contrast, antigen tests analyze stool samples to identify the presence of H. pylori antigens, indicating an active infection. Both testing methods are crucial for diagnosing H. pylori-related conditions, guiding treatment decisions, and monitoring the effectiveness of therapy, thereby driving demand in clinical and home testing markets.</p></p>
