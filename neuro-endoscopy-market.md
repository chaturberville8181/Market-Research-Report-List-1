<p><ul><li>Flexible Neuro-Endoscopy</li><li>Angled and Straight Rigid Neuro-Endoscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The neuro-endoscopy market is categorized into three main types: flexible neuro-endoscopes, angled rigid neuro-endoscopes, and straight rigid neuro-endoscopes. Flexible neuro-endoscopes are designed for intricate navigation in complex brain structures, providing better maneuverability. Angled rigid neuro-endoscopes offer specific visual access to targeted areas through angled optics, enhancing visibility during procedures. In contrast, straight rigid neuro-endoscopes provide a straightforward approach to surgical entry, making them suitable for less complicated access and procedures. Each type serves distinct clinical needs and applications in neurosurgery.</p></p>
