<p><p>The Commercial Heat Recovery Ventilation (HRV) Units market primarily features two types of heat exchangers: rotary heat exchangers and plate heat exchangers. Rotary heat exchangers utilize a rotating wheel to transfer heat between incoming and outgoing air streams, optimizing energy recovery. In contrast, plate heat exchangers consist of multiple plates stacked together, facilitating heat transfer through their surface area. Both types are designed to enhance energy efficiency, reduce operational costs, and improve indoor air quality in commercial settings.</p></p>
