<p><ul><li>Variable Air Volume (VAV)</li><li>Variable Refrigerant Volume (VRV)</li><li>Fan Control Unit (FCU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Air Conditioning Systems market includes several types, notably Variable Air Volume (VAV), Variable Refrigerant Volume (VRV), and Fan Control Units (FCU). VAV systems adjust airflow to maintain temperature, enhancing energy efficiency. VRV systems allow for individual zone control using a single refrigerant circuit, ideal for diverse building requirements. FCUs manage air distribution by regulating flow through fans, providing localized comfort. Together, these systems offer flexible solutions for varied commercial spaces, prioritizing energy efficiency and comfort.</p></p>
