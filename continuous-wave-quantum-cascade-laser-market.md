<p><p>The Continuous Wave Quantum Cascade Laser (CW QCL) market features three main types: Fabry–Perot lasers, Distributed Feedback (DFB) lasers, and tunable external cavity lasers. Fabry–Perot lasers provide broad emission spectra with high output power, suitable for various applications. DFB lasers offer single-mode operation, ensuring stable and precise wavelengths ideal for telecommunications and sensing. Tunable external cavity lasers enable fine-tuning of emission wavelengths, making them versatile for spectroscopy and research. Each type caters to specific requirements in industrial and scientific applications.</p></p>
