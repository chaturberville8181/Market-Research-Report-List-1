<p><p>The film release liners market is primarily categorized into three types: Polyolefin, BOPET (Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate), and BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene). Polyolefin liners offer good chemical resistance and flexibility, making them suitable for various applications. BOPET liners are known for their excellent dimensional stability and barrier properties, often used in high-quality graphics and labels. BOPP liners provide cost-effective solutions with good clarity and durability, commonly utilized in packaging and adhesive applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
