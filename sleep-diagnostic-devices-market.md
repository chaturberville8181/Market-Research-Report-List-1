<p><p>The sleep diagnostic devices market is segmented into several types based on the level of monitoring and patient environment. Full Attended Polysomnography (PSG) involves comprehensive overnight monitoring in a sleep lab, capturing detailed sleep data. Portable Unattended PSG allows patients to undergo testing at home with similar capabilities. Four-Channel Home Sleep Testing (HST) provides basic sleep analysis through fewer channels, while Three-Channel HST offers even simpler monitoring, focusing on essential parameters for diagnosing sleep disorders in a home setting.</p></p>
