<p><strong>The Degradable Mulch Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Starch-based</li><li>Starch Blend with PLA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The degradable mulch film market comprises primarily two types: starch-based and starch blend with PLA (polylactic acid). Starch-based mulch films are derived from natural starch sources, offering an environmentally friendly solution that decomposes in soil. In contrast, starch blends with PLA combine the renewable properties of starch with the bioplastic benefits of PLA, enhancing durability and flexibility while maintaining biodegradability. Both types cater to the agricultural sector’s demand for sustainable practices, reducing plastic waste and promoting soil health.</p></p>
