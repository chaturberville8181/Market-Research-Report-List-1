<p><p>The Xenon Lamp Spectrophotometer market encompasses various types of instruments designed for different analytical applications. UV-visible Spectrophotometers assess light absorption in the ultraviolet and visible range, commonly used for biochemical analysis. Infrared Spectrophotometers evaluate molecular vibrations, providing information on functional groups. Lastly, Visible Spectrophotometers focus on light in the visible spectrum, ideal for colorimetric assays and quantifying substances.</p></p>
