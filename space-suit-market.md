<p><ul><li>IVA (Intravehicular Activity) Suit</li><li>EVA (Extravehicular Activity) Suit</li><li>IEVA (Intra/Extravehicular Activity) Suit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The space suit market comprises three main types: IVA (Intravehicular Activity) suits, designed for use inside spacecraft, providing basic life support and protection in emergencies; EVA (Extravehicular Activity) suits, built for spacewalks, offering advanced thermal regulation, radiation shielding, and mobility in harsh space environments; and IEVA (Intra/Extravehicular Activity) suits, versatile designs capable of functioning both inside and outside spacecraft, balancing safety and flexibility. Each type caters to specific mission requirements, ensuring astronaut safety and operational efficiency in varying conditions.</p></p>
