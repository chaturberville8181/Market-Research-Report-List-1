Solvent-based label adhesives, on the other hand, use organic solvents, providing strong adhesion and durability, ideal for challenging environments. Both types cater to diverse applications in industries like packaging, logistics, and consumer goods, fulfilling specific performance and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
