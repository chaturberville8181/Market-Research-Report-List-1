<p><p>The cast iron check valves market is categorized primarily into two connection types: flanged connection and threaded connection. Flanged connection valves feature a flat surface and holes for bolting, ensuring a secure attachment to pipelines, making them ideal for high-pressure systems. Threaded connection valves utilize male or female threads for attachment, offering ease of installation and maintenance, typically in lower pressure applications. Both types serve to prevent backflow, contributing to the efficiency and safety of fluid transport systems.</p></p>
