<p><p>The Polymethyl Methacrylate (PMMA) film market comprises two major types: soft type and hard type. Soft PMMA films are flexible, offering excellent shock resistance and are often used in applications requiring bending or molding, such as in automotive and lighting sectors. Hard PMMA films, on the other hand, are rigid, providing superior optical clarity and impact resistance, making them suitable for uses in display technologies, protective covers, and high-visibility signage. Each type serves distinct application needs based on flexibility and durability.</p></p>
