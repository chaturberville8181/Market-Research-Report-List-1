<p><strong>The TOFD Ultrasonic Testing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Scanning</li><li>Sector Scanning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TOFD Ultrasonic Testing Machines use different scanning techniques to inspect materials for flaws. Electronic scanning employs phased-array technology, allowing for a rapid, comprehensive assessment of the test piece from various angles and depths, enhancing detection capabilities. In contrast, sector scanning utilizes a single transducer that moves in an arc, generating sector-shaped images of the test area. Both methods are essential for ensuring material integrity, but they cater to different requirements in terms of detail, speed, and coverage during inspections.</p></p>
