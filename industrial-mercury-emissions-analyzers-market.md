<p><strong>The Industrial Mercury Emissions Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption</li><li>Cold Atomic Fluorescence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Mercury Emissions Analyzers market comprises two primary types: Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption (CVAA) and Cold Atomic Fluorescence (CAF). CVAA technology utilizes the principles of atomic absorption to detect mercury by converting it into a vapor state, offering high sensitivity and reliability. In contrast, CAF employs fluorescence techniques to measure mercury levels, providing rapid and accurate results. Both methods are essential for monitoring mercury emissions in industrial processes, aiding compliance with environmental regulations and safeguarding public health.</p></p>
