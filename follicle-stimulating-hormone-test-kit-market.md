<p><p>The Follicle Stimulating Hormone (FSH) test kit market includes various types of assays, such as Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA) and Time-resolved Immunoassay (TRIA). CLIA utilizes a chemical reaction that emits light to quantify FSH levels, providing high sensitivity and accuracy. In contrast, TRIA relies on time-resolved fluorescence to detect hormone concentrations, offering rapid results with a low detection limit. These methodologies cater to diverse clinical and research needs in assessing fertility and reproductive health.</p></p>
