<p><p>The Zirconium target market includes two primary types: Planar Targets and Rotating Targets. Planar Targets are flat, stationary surfaces used in thin-film deposition processes, ideal for applications requiring uniform coatings. Rotating Targets are cylindrical and rotate during use, offering enhanced material utilization and longer lifespans, making them suitable for high-volume production. Both types are essential in industries like semiconductors, optics, and solar panels, where precise and efficient material deposition is critical for performance and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
