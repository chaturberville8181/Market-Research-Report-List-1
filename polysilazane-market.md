<p><strong>The Polysilazane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Organic Polysilazanes (OPSZ)</li><li>Perhydropolysilazanes (PHPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polysilazane market comprises two primary types: Organic Polysilazanes (OPSZ) and Perhydropolysilazanes (PHPS). OPSZ contains organic groups like methyl or phenyl, offering flexibility, adhesion, and compatibility with organic materials, making it suitable for coatings, adhesives, and composites. PHPS, with a higher hydrogen content, forms dense, inorganic silica-like films upon curing, ideal for protective coatings, anti-reflective layers, and semiconductor applications. Both types are valued for their thermal stability, chemical resistance, and versatility in advanced material technologies.</p></p>
