<p><p>The Accumulation Conveyor market consists of Zero Pressure Accumulation Conveyors and Low Pressure Accumulation Conveyors. Zero Pressure Accumulation Conveyors ensure products do not touch each other while being transported, preventing damage and allowing for precise control. In contrast, Low Pressure Accumulation Conveyors apply minimal pressure on products, facilitating smoother movements while still managing product flow. Both types enhance operational efficiency in various industries by optimizing material handling, reducing product collisions, and improving overall logistics performance.</p></p>
