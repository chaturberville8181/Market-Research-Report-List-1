<p><p>The High Purity Isobutylene (HPIB) market includes several production methods. The MTBE Decomposition Method involves breaking down methyl tert-butyl ether to extract isobutylene. The C4 Fraction Separation Method separates C4 hydrocarbons from crude oil, refining them to isolate isobutylene. The Isobutane Dehydrogenation Method converts isobutane into isobutylene through a dehydrogenation process. Other methods may include various catalytic processes or innovative technologies for HPIB production, catering to diverse industrial applications. These methods are pivotal for ensuring high purity and efficiency in isobutylene supply.</p></p>
