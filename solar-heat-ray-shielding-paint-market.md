<p><p>The Solar Heat-ray Shielding Paint market is categorized into two main types: Heat Ray Reflection and Heat Ray Absorption. Heat Ray Reflection paints are designed to reflect sunlight and reduce heat buildup, enhancing energy efficiency in buildings. In contrast, Heat Ray Absorption paints absorb solar energy, preventing it from penetrating into structures, thereby maintaining cooler indoor temperatures. Both types play a crucial role in energy conservation, climate control, and improving living conditions in warm climates by reducing reliance on air conditioning systems.</p></p>
