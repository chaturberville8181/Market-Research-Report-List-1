<p><ul><li>X Band Radar</li><li>S Band Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Navigational radar is essential for safe maritime operations, with various market types including X Band and S Band radar. X Band radar operates in the 8-12 GHz frequency range, providing high-resolution images and effective detection of small objects, making it ideal for close-range navigation and coastal applications. In contrast, S Band radar, operating at 2-4 GHz, offers greater range and better performance in adverse weather, making it suited for open ocean navigation and vessel traffic monitoring. Together, they enhance maritime safety and efficiency.</p></p>
