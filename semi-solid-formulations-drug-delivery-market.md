<p><p>The semi-solid formulations drug delivery market encompasses various types including creams, ointments, lotions, gels, and pastes. Creams combine oil and water, providing hydration while delivering active ingredients. Ointments, oil-based and thicker, offer longer-lasting moisture and protection. Lotions are lighter, easily spreadable emulsions ideal for large surface areas. Gels consist of water-based components, offering a cooling effect and quick absorption. Pastes are thicker, providing a barrier while offering targeted drug delivery, particularly for localized treatment. Each formulation type serves distinct therapeutic purposes and enhances patient compliance.</p></p>
