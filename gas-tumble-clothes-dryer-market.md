<p><p>The gas tumble clothes dryer market comprises two primary types: condensing dryers and vented dryers. Condensing dryers use a closed-loop system to extract moisture from clothes, condensing it into water, which can be drained or emptied manually. This type is ideal for homes without external venting options. Vented dryers, on the other hand, expel moist air directly outside through a duct. They tend to be faster and more energy-efficient but require proper installation with access to the exterior.</p></p>
