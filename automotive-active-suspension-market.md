<p><p>The automotive active suspension market comprises various technologies aimed at enhancing vehicle ride quality and handling. Air suspension utilizes air springs to adjust stiffness and damping dynamically, providing adaptable comfort. Hydraulic suspension relies on fluid mechanisms for real-time adjustment, offering stability and responsiveness. Electromagnetic suspension employs magnetic forces to control ride height and performance, enabling precision handling. Electro-hydraulic suspension combines electrical and hydraulic components for improved performance, providing a balance between comfort and agility in various driving conditions.</p></p>
