<p><p>The portable medical ventilators market includes manual and mechanical ventilators. Manual ventilators, often bag-mask devices, are operated by healthcare providers to assist or control a patient's breathing during emergencies or non-invasive procedures. In contrast, mechanical ventilators are automated systems that support or replace spontaneous breathing, typically used in critical care settings. Both types enhance patient management, offering flexibility in various environments, from hospitals to remote locations, ensuring respiratory support when needed.</p></p>
