Casting products, such as plaster and fiberglass casts, are designed to immobilize broken bones and support healing. Splinting products, on the other hand, provide temporary stabilization for fractures, sprains, or soft tissue injuries, allowing for mobility while protecting the affected area. Both product types are essential in orthopedic and trauma care, catering to various patient needs in the healthcare sector.</p></p>
