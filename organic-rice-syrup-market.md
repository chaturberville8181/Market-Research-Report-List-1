<p><p>The organic rice syrup market is segmented into two main types: brown rice and white rice syrups. Brown rice syrup, made from whole grain rice, retains more nutrients and has a distinct, nutty flavor. It is often preferred for its health benefits and lower glycemic index. In contrast, white rice syrup is derived from processed white rice, resulting in a milder taste and a sweeter profile. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences, impacting their applications in food products and health considerations.</p></p>
