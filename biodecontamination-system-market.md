<p><p>The biodecontamination system market is primarily segmented into chamber decontamination and room decontamination types. Chamber decontamination involves the use of enclosed spaces to eliminate contaminants from equipment and materials through controlled methods like vaporized hydrogen peroxide. Room decontamination, on the other hand, focuses on disinfecting larger areas, such as healthcare facilities, utilizing techniques like aerosolized disinfectants or fogging systems. Both types aim to enhance infection control and ensure safety in environments susceptible to biological hazards.</p></p>
