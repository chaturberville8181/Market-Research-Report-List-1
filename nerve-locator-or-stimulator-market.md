<p><p>The nerve locator or stimulator market is categorized into two main types: fixed and variable nerve locators/stimulators. Fixed nerve locators/stimulators provide consistent electrical stimulation at predetermined settings, ensuring reliability and ease of use during surgical procedures. In contrast, variable nerve locators/stimulators allow practitioners to adjust the stimulation parameters according to specific patient needs, enhancing flexibility and precision in nerve identification and monitoring. Both types are essential for improving surgical outcomes by minimizing nerve damage and facilitating better patient recovery.</p></p>
