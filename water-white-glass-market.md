<p><p>The Water White Glass market comprises primarily two types: Rolled Glass and Float Glass. Rolled Glass is produced through a process that involves flattening molten glass, offering a textured surface suitable for various applications, including decorative and architectural uses. In contrast, Float Glass is manufactured by floating molten glass on molten tin, resulting in a smooth and clear finish, ideal for windows and glazing. Both types cater to distinct market needs, enhancing aesthetic appeal and functional performance in construction and design.</p></p>
