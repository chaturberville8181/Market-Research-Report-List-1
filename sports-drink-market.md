<p><strong>The Sports Drink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hypotonic Sports Drink</li><li>Isotonic Sports Drink</li><li>Hypertonic Sports Drink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sports drink market is categorized into three main types based on osmolarity: hypotonic, isotonic, and hypertonic drinks. Hypotonic sports drinks contain lower concentrations of carbohydrates and electrolytes, providing quick hydration and replenishment without heavy calorie load, ideal for low-intensity activities. Isotonic drinks have balanced electrolyte and carbohydrate levels, effectively replenishing fluids and energy during moderate to intense exercise. Hypertonic drinks offer higher carbohydrate concentrations, making them suitable for post-exercise recovery to restore glycogen levels. Each serves distinct hydration and energy needs for athletes.</p></p>
