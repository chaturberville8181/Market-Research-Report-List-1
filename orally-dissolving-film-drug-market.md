<p><strong>The Orally Dissolving Film Drug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Orodispersible Film</li><li>Oromucosal Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orally dissolving film drug market comprises two main types: orodispersible films and oromucosal films. Orodispersible films are designed to dissolve quickly in saliva, allowing for rapid drug release and absorption, making them ideal for patients with difficulty swallowing. Oromucosal films, on the other hand, adhere to oral mucosal tissues, facilitating localized or systemic drug delivery. Both types offer advantages such as enhanced bioavailability, improved patient compliance, and a non-invasive alternative to traditional oral dosage forms.</p></p>
