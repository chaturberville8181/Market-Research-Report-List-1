<p><strong>The Automated Mooring Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mooring Lines</li><li>No Mooring Lines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated Mooring Systems (AMS) can be categorized primarily into two types: mooring lines and no mooring lines. </p><p>Mooring lines systems utilize traditional ropes or cables to secure vessels, enhancing safety and stability during docking. In contrast, no mooring lines systems leverage advanced technologies, such as dynamic positioning and suction-based mechanisms, to eliminate the need for physical lines, streamlining the mooring process. Both types aim to optimize efficiency, reduce labor costs, and improve operational safety in marine environments.</p></p>
