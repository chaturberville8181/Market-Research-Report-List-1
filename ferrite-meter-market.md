<p><strong>The Ferrite Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Probe</li><li>Diameter Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ferrite Meter market is segmented into two main types: area probes and diameter probes. Area probes measure the ferrite content in a specific surface area, making them suitable for inspecting larger components or materials with varying thicknesses. In contrast, diameter probes are designed to determine ferrite levels in cylindrical objects or tubing, allowing for precise measurements in confined spaces. Both probe types cater to various industrial applications, ensuring quality control and compliance in materials such as stainless steel.</p></p>
