<p><p>In-memory OLAP databases enhance data processing speeds for analytical tasks. MOLAP (Multidimensional OLAP) stores data in a cube format, allowing rapid access for complex queries. DOLAP (Desktop OLAP) is designed for individual users, offering offline data processing. SOLAP (Spatial OLAP) integrates spatial data for geographic information analysis, aiding in decision-making. Other types, like HOLAP (Hybrid OLAP), combine elements of MOLAP and ROLAP (Relational OLAP) to optimize performance and scalability, catering to diverse analytical needs and user environments.</p></p>
