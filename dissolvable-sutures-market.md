<p><ul><li>Natural Dissolvable Sutures</li><li>Synthetic Dissolvable Sutures</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dissolvable sutures market includes two main types: natural and synthetic. Natural dissolvable sutures are made from biological materials, such as collagen or gut, which naturally degrade in the body over time. Synthetic dissolvable sutures, on the other hand, are engineered from materials like polyglycolic acid or polylactic acid, offering controlled absorption and varying tensile strength. Both types are used in surgical procedures to provide secure wound closure while minimizing the need for additional sutures removal.</p></p>
