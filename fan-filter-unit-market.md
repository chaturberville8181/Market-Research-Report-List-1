<p><p>The Fan Filter Unit (FFU) market primarily consists of two types: FFUs with HEPA filters and those with ULPA filters. HEPA filter units are designed to capture particles as small as 0.3 microns, making them suitable for environments like laboratories and cleanrooms. In contrast, ULPA filter units provide even higher efficiency, capturing particles as small as 0.12 microns, which is essential for critical applications in pharmaceuticals and semiconductor manufacturing. Both types contribute significantly to maintaining air quality in controlled environments.</p></p>
