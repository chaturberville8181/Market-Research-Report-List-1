<p><p>The security thermal camera market is primarily categorized into bullet cameras and PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) cameras. Bullet cameras are compact, cylindrical devices typically mounted on walls, offering a fixed field of view ideal for monitoring specific areas. In contrast, PTZ cameras provide greater flexibility, allowing operators to pan, tilt, and zoom for comprehensive surveillance over larger areas. Both types utilize thermal imaging technology to detect heat signatures, enhancing visibility in low-light and challenging environmental conditions, making them essential for security applications.</p></p>
