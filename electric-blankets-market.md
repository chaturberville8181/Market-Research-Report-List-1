<p><p>The electric blankets market is primarily divided into two types: underblankets and overblankets. Underblankets are designed to be placed on top of the mattress, providing warmth beneath the bedding. They protect the mattress and offer gentle heat directly to the sleeper. Overblankets, on the other hand, are used as traditional blankets, offering warmth on top of the sheets. Both types utilize electrical heating elements, catering to different preferences for comfort and warmth during cold weather.</p></p>
