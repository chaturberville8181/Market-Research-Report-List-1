<p><strong>The Fireproofing Hydraulic Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HFA</li><li>HFB</li><li>HFC</li><li>HFD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fireproofing hydraulic fluid market is categorized into four main types: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are water-based solutions containing emulsifiers, offering good fire resistance but limited lubrication. HFB fluids are also water-containing but have additives that enhance lubrication properties. HFC fluids are water-containing and formulated with fire-resistant additives suitable for higher temperatures. HFD fluids are non-water-containing and provide superior fire resistance, often used in extreme conditions. Each type serves specific applications in various industrial settings.</p></p>
