<p><p>The Industrial Air Pollution Control Solutions market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Electrostatic Precipitators (ESP)**: These devices remove particles from exhaust gases using electrical charges.</p><p>2. **Flue Gas Desulfurization (FGD)**: This technology eliminates sulfur dioxide emissions from power plant flue gases.</p><p>3. **Scrubbers**: These systems neutralize acidic gases and particulates in industrial emissions.</p><p>4. **Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR)**: SCR technology reduces nitrogen oxides using a catalyst and a reducing agent.</p><p>5. **Fabric Filters**: Utilized for particulate collection, they trap dust and contaminants from industrial exhaust streams.</p></p>
