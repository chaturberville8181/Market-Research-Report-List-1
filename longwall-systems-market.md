<p><ul><li>Double-Ended Ranging Drum Shearer</li><li>Single-Ended Ranging Drum Shearer</li><li>Single-Ended Fixed Drum Shearer</li><li>Three-Drum Shearer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Longwall systems for coal mining utilize various shearer types based on operational needs. The Double-Ended Ranging Drum Shearer features drums on both ends for efficient cutting and flexibility. The Single-Ended Ranging Drum Shearer is designed with a drum on one end for precise mining operations. The Single-Ended Fixed Drum Shearer has a stationary drum that provides stability but less versatility. Lastly, the Three-Drum Shearer incorporates three drums allowing for higher production rates and improved coal recovery in a single pass.</p></p>
