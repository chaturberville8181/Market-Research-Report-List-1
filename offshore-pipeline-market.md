<p><strong>The Offshore Pipeline Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Export Line</li><li>Transport Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offshore pipeline market consists of two primary types: export lines and transport lines. Export lines are designed to transport hydrocarbons, such as oil and gas, from offshore production facilities directly to onshore processing or distribution points, facilitating international trade. In contrast, transport lines move hydrocarbons between various offshore sites, including from one platform to another or to a central processing facility, ensuring efficient resource management within offshore operations. Both types are crucial for the reliability and efficiency of energy supply chains.</p></p>
