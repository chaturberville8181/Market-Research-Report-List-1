<p><p>The **Automotive High Voltage Cables Market** includes **Shielded Cables** and **Unshielded Cables**, essential for electric and hybrid vehicles. Shielded cables feature protective layers to minimize electromagnetic interference, ensuring reliable signal transmission and safety in high-voltage systems. Unshielded cables lack this protection, making them lighter and more cost-effective but less suitable for environments with high interference. Both types are critical for powering electric drivetrains, batteries, and charging systems, with shielded cables preferred for advanced applications requiring enhanced durability and performance.</p></p>
