<p><p>The bariatric surgical procedures market includes various types designed to help individuals achieve weight loss. Gastric bypass surgery reroutes the digestive tract, promoting significant weight loss. Adjustable gastric banding involves placing an inflatable band around the stomach, restricting food intake. Gastric sleeve surgery removes a portion of the stomach, creating a smaller pouch. Duodenal switch surgery combines sleeve and bypass techniques, enhancing weight loss and metabolic benefits. Other procedures may include balloon therapies and less common surgeries tailored to individual needs.</p></p>
