<p><p>Intraoperative imaging systems encompass technologies that provide real-time visualization during surgical procedures. Intraoperative CT offers high-resolution cross-sectional images, aiding in precise localization and tumor resection. Intraoperative MRI allows for detailed brain and soft tissue imaging, enhancing neurosurgical interventions. Intraoperative ultrasound utilizes sound waves to produce live images of soft tissues, enabling surgeons to assess anatomical structures and guide interventions effectively. Together, these modalities enhance surgical precision and patient outcomes by facilitating informed decision-making during procedures.</p></p>
