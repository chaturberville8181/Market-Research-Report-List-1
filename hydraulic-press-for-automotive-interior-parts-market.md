<p><p>The hydraulic press for automotive interior parts market can be categorized into two main types: frame type and four-column type. Frame-type hydraulic presses feature a robust structure that provides uniform pressure and is ideal for simple applications, such as molding and stamping. On the other hand, four-column type presses offer enhanced stability and precision, making them suitable for more complex tasks that require higher tonnage and accuracy, such as deep drawing and intricate part shaping in automotive interiors.</p></p>
