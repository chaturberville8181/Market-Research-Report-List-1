<p><p>The body armor market comprises various types, including steel, UHMWPE (ultra-high-molecular-weight polyethylene), aramid fibers, composite ceramics, and others. Steel armor offers durability and resistance but is heavier. UHMWPE is lightweight, providing high protection with greater mobility. Aramid fibers, like Kevlar, are known for their strength and flexibility. Composite ceramics blend materials for effective impact resistance while minimizing weight. The "others" category encompasses advanced or niche materials, catering to specialized applications in both military and civilian sectors.</p></p>
