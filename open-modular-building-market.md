<p><p>The Open Modular Building Market encompasses two main types: Permanent Modular and Relocatable Modular structures. Permanent Modular buildings are constructed off-site and transported for permanent installation, offering durability and architectural flexibility. They are ideal for educational, commercial, and healthcare facilities. In contrast, Relocatable Modular buildings are designed for temporary use, providing flexible space solutions that can be easily moved and reconfigured as needs change. Both types enhance efficiency in construction while addressing diverse spatial demands.</p></p>
