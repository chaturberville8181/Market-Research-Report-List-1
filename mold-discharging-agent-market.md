<p><strong>The Mold Discharging Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Release Agent</li><li>External Release Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mold discharging agent market comprises two primary types: internal and external release agents. Internal release agents are integrated within the material during production, enhancing mold release properties and improving surface quality. In contrast, external release agents are applied directly onto the mold's surface, creating a barrier that facilitates the easy removal of the finished product. Both agents are essential in various manufacturing processes, particularly in composites and plastics, to reduce defects and optimize production efficiency.</p></p>
