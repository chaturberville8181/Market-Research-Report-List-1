<p><p>The paving geotextiles market consists of two primary types: woven and non-woven fibers. Woven geotextiles are crafted from interlaced fibers, providing high strength and structural support, making them ideal for reinforcement in paved surfaces. Non-woven geotextiles, on the other hand, are made from bonded fibers, offering excellent drainage and filtration properties. These materials are essential in construction and civil engineering, enhancing the stability and longevity of roadways and pavements while preventing soil erosion and ensuring efficient water management.</p></p>
