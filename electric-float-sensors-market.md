<p><p>Electric float sensors are crucial for fluid level detection in various industries. The top-mounted type is installed on the tank's upper part, allowing it to measure liquid levels from above, ideal for narrow or high tanks where side access is limited. In contrast, side-mounted sensors are fixed to the tank's side, providing a more direct measurement of liquid levels and suitable for larger tanks. Both types serve to ensure accurate fluid management and automation in diverse applications.</p></p>
