<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Medium Voltage Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Type</li><li>Oil Immersed Type</li><li>VPI (Vacuum Pressure Impregnated Type)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medium voltage transformers market comprises three main types: Dry Type, Oil Immersed Type, and VPI (Vacuum Pressure Impregnated Type). Dry Type transformers utilize air cooling, making them ideal for indoor applications with limited flammability. Oil Immersed transformers, filled with insulating oil, are effective for outdoor installations and larger capacities, offering better cooling. VPI transformers are created by impregnating insulation materials under vacuum, enhancing insulation quality and moisture resistance. Each type serves specific operational needs and installation environments.</p></p>
