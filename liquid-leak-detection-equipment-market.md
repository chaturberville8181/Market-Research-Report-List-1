<p><ul><li>Invasive Leak Detection</li><li>Non-Invasive Leak Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid leak detection equipment market is categorized into two main types: invasive and non-invasive leak detection. Invasive leak detection involves physically accessing the infrastructure, such as pipes or tanks, to identify leaks through direct inspection or testing methods. Non-invasive leak detection, on the other hand, utilizes external technologies like acoustic, thermal, or pressure sensors to detect leaks without disturbing the system. Both methods offer unique advantages, catering to various industrial applications and promoting efficient maintenance and safety practices.</p></p>
