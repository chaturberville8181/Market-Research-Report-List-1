<p><p>The optical fiber transceiver market encompasses various types, including optical receivers, optical transmitters, and optical transponders. Optical receivers convert incoming optical signals into electrical signals for processing, ensuring data integrity and clarity. Optical transmitters, on the other hand, send data by converting electrical signals into optical signals for transmission over fiber networks. Optical transponders integrate both functions, facilitating bi-directional communication by receiving and transmitting signals, thus serving as a crucial component in high-speed optical networks.</p></p>
