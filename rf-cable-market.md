<p><ul><li>Coaxial Cable</li><li>Fiber-Optic Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF cable market includes two primary types: coaxial cable and fiber-optic cable. Coaxial cables are widely used for transmitting radio frequency signals, featuring an insulated copper conductor surrounded by a tubular insulating layer and a conductive shield. They are popular in television and internet applications. Fiber-optic cables, on the other hand, use glass or plastic fibers to transmit data as light signals, offering higher bandwidth and faster speeds over longer distances. Both types cater to diverse communication needs in various industries.</p></p>
