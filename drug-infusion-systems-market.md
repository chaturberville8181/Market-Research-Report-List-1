<p><p>The drug infusion systems market encompasses several types, including ambulatory infusion systems, implantable infusion systems, and syringe infusion systems. Ambulatory infusion systems are portable devices allowing patients to receive therapy while on the move. Implantable infusion systems are surgically placed within the body to deliver medication directly and continuously. Syringe infusion systems utilize syringes to precisely administer medication at controlled rates, often used in hospitals. Each type caters to specific patient needs and treatment protocols, enhancing therapeutic outcomes.</p></p>
