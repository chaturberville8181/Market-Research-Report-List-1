<p><p>The wind turbine bolt tensioner market comprises two main types: single-stage and multi-stage tensioners. Single-stage tensioners apply force in a single operation to achieve bolt tension, suitable for smaller installations or simpler applications. In contrast, multi-stage tensioners utilize a series of stages to incrementally apply tension, providing greater control and precision. This makes them ideal for larger turbines or complex installations, where optimal bolt tension is crucial for safety and performance, enhancing overall efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
