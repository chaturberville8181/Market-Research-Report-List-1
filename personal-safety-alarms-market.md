<p><p>Personal safety alarms are categorized into two main types: active alarms and passive alarms. Active alarms require user initiation, such as pressing a button or pulling a cord, to trigger a loud sound or alert that can attract attention. In contrast, passive alarms operate automatically, detecting unusual activity or emergencies (like falls) to emit alerts without user action. These classifications cater to diverse consumer needs, enhancing personal security in various situations by offering either reactive or proactive safety solutions.</p></p>
