<p><p>Continuous Renal Replacement Therapy (CRRT) machines are specialized medical devices used to provide renal support for patients with acute kidney injury or chronic kidney disease, especially in critical care settings. Technological advancements, such as improved filtration systems and automation, are enhancing the efficiency and outcomes of CRRT.
