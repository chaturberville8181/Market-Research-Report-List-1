<p><ul><li>Physical Cell Disruption Method</li><li>Reagent Based Cell Disruption Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cell disruption method market is categorized into two main types: physical and reagent-based methods. </p><p>Physical cell disruption methods utilize mechanical techniques, such as homogenization, sonication, or freeze-thaw cycles, to break down cell membranes and release cellular contents. </p><p>In contrast, reagent-based cell disruption methods rely on the use of chemical agents or enzymes to lyse cells, facilitating the extraction of biomolecules. Both markets serve crucial roles in biotechnology, pharmaceuticals, and research, each offering unique advantages based on application requirements.</p></p>
