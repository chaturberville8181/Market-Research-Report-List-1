<p><strong>The Particle Board Furniture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laminated Particle Board</li><li>Veneered Particle Board</li><li>Cement Bonded Particle Board</li><li>Melamine Particle Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The particle board furniture market features various types tailored for diverse needs. Laminated particle board is coated with a plastic film for durability and aesthetic appeal. Veneered particle board has a thin layer of natural wood veneer, enhancing its visual quality. Cement bonded particle board is reinforced with cement, offering moisture resistance and strength, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Melamine particle board is coated with melamine resin, providing scratch and heat resistance, making it popular for modern furniture designs. Each type serves specific functionalities and aesthetic preferences.</p></p>
