<p><p>The semi-trailer suspension market includes various types designed for different load capacities and road conditions. **Leaf Spring Suspension** uses stacked metal strips for durability and cost-effectiveness, ideal for heavy loads. **Torsion Suspension** employs rubber cords for smoother rides and reduced maintenance, suitable for lighter loads. **Heavy Duty Adjustable Suspension** offers flexibility for varying load weights, ensuring stability. **Air Ride Suspension** uses airbags for superior comfort, load balancing, and reduced wear, preferred for high-value or fragile cargo. Each type caters to specific operational needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
