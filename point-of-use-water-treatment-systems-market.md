<p><p>Point of Use Water Treatment Systems encompass various technologies designed to improve water quality at the consumption point. Reverse Osmosis uses semi-permeable membranes to remove dissolved impurities. Distillation involves boiling water to separate contaminants, allowing purified vapor to condense. Disinfection employs methods like UV light or chemical agents to eliminate pathogens. Filtration utilizes physical barriers, such as activated carbon or ceramic filters, to capture sediments and pollutants, ensuring safe drinking water directly at the tap.</p></p>
