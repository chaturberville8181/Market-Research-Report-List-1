<p><strong>The Ultra-Clear Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Glass</li><li>Float Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra-Clear Glass market primarily includes two types: Rolled Glass and Float Glass. Rolled Glass is produced by passing molten glass between rollers, creating a uniform thickness and texture suitable for various applications, particularly in architectural designs. Float Glass, on the other hand, is made by floating molten glass on molten tin, resulting in a smooth, flawless surface ideal for high-clarity requirements. Both types are valued for their optical clarity and aesthetic appeal, serving sectors like construction and automotive.</p></p>
