<p><p>Stone cladding systems involve covering surfaces with stone materials for aesthetic and protective purposes. The market is divided into **Natural Stone** and **Technical Stone**. Natural Stone cladding uses quarried materials like granite, marble, and limestone, offering unique textures and durability. Technical Stone, or engineered stone, is manufactured using crushed stone bound with resins, providing consistent patterns, lighter weight, and enhanced flexibility. Both types cater to architectural and design needs, balancing aesthetics, functionality, and cost.</p></p>
