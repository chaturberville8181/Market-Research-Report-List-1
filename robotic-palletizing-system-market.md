<p><p>The robotic palletizing system market comprises two main types: floor/low-level palletizers and high-level palletizers. Floor/low-level palletizers are designed for applications requiring low height stacking, ideal for smaller products or facilities with limited vertical space. In contrast, high-level palletizers stack products at greater heights, suitable for high-volume operations and larger goods.
