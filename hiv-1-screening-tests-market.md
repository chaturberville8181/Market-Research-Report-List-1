<p><p>The HIV-1 screening tests market comprises various types, including ELISA, rapid tests, and home access dried blood spot tests. ELISA (enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay) is a laboratory-based method that detects antibodies in blood samples, offering high sensitivity. Rapid tests provide quick results, often within minutes, making them suitable for point-of-care settings. Home access dried blood spot tests enable individuals to collect their own samples for later analysis, enhancing privacy and accessibility, thereby expanding screening options for HIV-1 detection.</p></p>
