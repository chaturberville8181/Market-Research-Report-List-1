<p><ul><li>Semi-Active</li><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multifunctional orthopedic surgical robot market can be categorized into three types: semi-active, active, and passive. Semi-active robots assist surgeons by providing haptic feedback and real-time guidance, enhancing precision. Active robots have autonomous capabilities, enabling them to perform specific surgical tasks independently while adapting to the surgical environment. Passive robots, on the other hand, do not have significant automation features; they offer stabilization and positioning support, allowing surgeons to execute procedures with improved accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
