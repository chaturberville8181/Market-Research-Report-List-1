<p><p>Carousel storage systems are automated solutions designed to enhance efficiency in inventory management. Horizontal carousel storage systems consist of an oval track that rotates horizontally to bring items to the operator, ideal for storing larger, wider items. In contrast, vertical carousel storage systems utilize a vertical arrangement, where items are stored in a series of shelves that rotate up and down, maximizing space and improving accessibility for smaller items. Both systems streamline operations and optimize storage capacity in various industries.</p></p>
