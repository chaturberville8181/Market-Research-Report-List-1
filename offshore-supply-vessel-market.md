<p><p>The Offshore Supply Vessel (OSV) market serves two primary applications: shallow water and deep water operations. In shallow water, OSVs support activities like drilling, construction, and maintenance in relatively accessible offshore areas, requiring vessels with lower draft and maneuverability. In deep water, OSVs handle complex tasks such as subsea installations, equipment transport, and support for ultra-deepwater drilling rigs, demanding advanced technology, larger capacities, and enhanced stability. Both markets are vital for offshore energy exploration and production, with varying operational challenges and vessel specifications.</p></p>
