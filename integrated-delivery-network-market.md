<p><strong>The Integrated Delivery Network Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Integration</li><li>Horizontal Integration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Integrated Delivery Networks (IDNs) can be categorized into vertical and horizontal integration types. Vertical integration involves a single organization controlling multiple levels of the healthcare delivery system, such as hospitals, outpatient services, and specialty care, enabling seamless coordination and comprehensive patient care. In contrast, horizontal integration occurs when organizations at the same level, like hospitals or clinics, merge or collaborate to expand their reach, share resources, and enhance service offerings while maintaining their distinct operational identities. Both strategies aim to improve efficiency and patient outcomes.</p></p>
