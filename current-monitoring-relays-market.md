<p><ul><li>Under-Current Detection</li><li>Over-Current Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Current Monitoring Relays market is primarily categorized into two types: Under-Current Detection and Over-Current Detection. Under-Current Detection relays safeguard equipment by monitoring for unexpected drops in current, which can indicate faults like equipment failure or overload conditions. Conversely, Over-Current Detection relays are designed to prevent damage from excessive current flow by tripping the circuit when predefined thresholds are exceeded. Both types are crucial for optimizing operational efficiency and ensuring the protection of electrical systems in various industries.</p></p>
