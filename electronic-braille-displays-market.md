<p><p>The Electronic Braille Displays market is divided into two primary types: Compact Braille Cells and Dispersible Braille Cells. Compact Braille Cells feature 30-80 Braille cells, making them portable and ideal for personal use, such as reading e-books or navigating smartphones. In contrast, Dispersible Braille Cells comprise 80-360 cells, offering a larger reading surface suitable for educational and professional environments, enabling users to access more extensive content simultaneously. Both types enhance accessibility for individuals with visual impairments.</p></p>
