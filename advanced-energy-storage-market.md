<p><p>The advanced energy storage market includes various technologies. Pumped hydro storage utilizes gravitational potential energy, storing water in elevated reservoirs to generate electricity when needed. Battery storage employs chemical processes to store and discharge electricity, offering flexibility and quick response times. Flywheel storage uses kinetic energy, spinning a rotor to release energy rapidly. Thermal storage captures heat for later use, often in concentrated solar power systems. Other technologies may include compressed air and supercapacitors, enhancing grid stability and renewable energy integration.</p></p>
