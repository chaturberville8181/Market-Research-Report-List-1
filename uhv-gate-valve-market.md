<p><p>The UHV (Ultra High Vacuum) gate valve market is primarily segmented into two main types: manual valves and pneumatic valves. Manual valves require human operation to open or close, offering simplicity and reliability in low-frequency operations. In contrast, pneumatic valves use compressed air or gas for actuation, enabling automated control and faster response times, which is ideal for high-frequency applications. Both types are crucial for maintaining vacuum integrity in various industrial and scientific environments, each serving specific operational needs.</p></p>
