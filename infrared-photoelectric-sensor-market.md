<p><strong>The Infrared Photoelectric Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared photoelectric sensor market is divided into two main types: active and passive sensors. Active infrared sensors emit modulated IR light beams and detect interruptions or reflections, making them ideal for object detection and presence sensing. Conversely, passive infrared sensors do not emit any light but instead detect ambient infrared radiation from objects, typically used in motion detection and security applications. Each type serves specific functions and industries, contributing to market diversity and technological advancements.</p></p>
