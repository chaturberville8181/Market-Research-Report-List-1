<p><ul><li>Internal Radius Gauges</li><li>External Radius Gauges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radius gauges market is divided into two main types: internal radius gauges and external radius gauges. Internal radius gauges are designed to measure the curvature or radius of inside surfaces, such as pipes or holes, ensuring precision in engineering and manufacturing applications. External radius gauges, on the other hand, are used to measure the radius of outside surfaces, such as the edges of components or molds. Both types are essential tools in quality control, fabrication, and machining processes.</p></p>
