<p><p>The Fire Retardant ABS market is categorized into two main types: Non-halogen Type and Halogen Type. Non-halogen type fire retardants use alternative chemicals to reduce flammability, offering a more environmentally friendly option with lower toxicity during combustion. On the other hand, Halogen type fire retardants, typically involving bromine or chlorine compounds, are effective in enhancing fire resistance but can release harmful substances when ignited. Both types cater to various industries, balancing performance and environmental considerations in fire safety applications.</p></p>
